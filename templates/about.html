By leveraging Random Forest algorithms and comprehensive feature engineering, we provide accurate price predictions that can guide real estate transactions, investment strategies, and financial planning decisions.

üî¨ How It Works

Our prediction model is powered by a Random Forest algorithm, trained on comprehensive real estate data. The model analyzes 10 critical property features to generate accurate price predictions:

  • Overall Quality (1-10): Material and finish quality rating of the property
  • Above Grade Living Area: Living space above ground level in square feet
  • Garage Capacity: Number of cars the garage can accommodate
  • Garage Area: Total garage square footage
  • First Floor Area: Square footage of the first floor
  • Total Basement Area: Complete basement square footage
  • Lot Area: Total property lot size
  • Finished Basement: Type 1 finished basement area
  • Full Bathrooms: Number of complete bathrooms above grade
  • Years Since Remodel: Time elapsed since last major renovation

These features undergo advanced preprocessing including log transformations, scaling, and imputation before being fed into our trained Random Forest model for optimal prediction accuracy.

üìà Model Performance

Our Random Forest model has been meticulously trained on thousands of real estate transactions and achieves high accuracy in price predictions. The ensemble nature of Random Forest helps:

  • Reduce Overfitting: Multiple decision trees prevent memorization of training data
  • Handle Non-linearity: Captures complex relationships between features
  • Robust Predictions: Less sensitive to outliers and noisy data
  • Feature Importance: Identifies which property attributes matter most

The model continuously considers all 10 property attributes with their interactions to provide reliable valuations that reflect current market conditions and property characteristics.
