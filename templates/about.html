üî¨ How It Works

Our prediction model is trained on comprehensive real estate data, analyzing various property features including:

  • Overall Quality: Material and finish quality rating
  • Living Area: Above grade living space in square feet
  • Garage Features: Size and capacity
  • Basement Details: Total and finished basement area
  • Floor Space: First floor and total lot area
  • Bathrooms: Number of full bathrooms
  • Property Age: Years since last remodel

These factors are processed through a trained machine learning model to predict house prices with high accuracy.
